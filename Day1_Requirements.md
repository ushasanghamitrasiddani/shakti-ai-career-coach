# Shakti AI – Career Reboot for Women Returning to Work

## Problem Statement

Many women take career breaks due to childcare, family responsibilities, or relocation. After several years away from work, they face:

- Loss of confidence
- Outdated skills
- Resume gaps
- Difficulty identifying suitable jobs
- Interview anxiety
- Lack of personalized guidance

Shakti AI is an AI-powered Career Restart Coach that helps women return to work through skill assessment, learning roadmaps, resume building, interview preparation, and job recommendations.

## User Journey

1. User visits Shakti AI website
2. User creates a profile
3. User answers career assessment questions
4. AI analyzes skills and career history
5. AI identifies transferable skills
6. AI generates a personalized learning roadmap
7. AI creates a resume draft
8. User practices mock interviews
9. AI suggests suitable job roles
10. User tracks progress on dashboard
## Features List

### MVP Features
